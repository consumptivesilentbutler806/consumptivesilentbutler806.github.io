🎧 Setting Up Your DAW

After you finish the installation, you must connect the software to your music app. Most professional audio programs, also called a Digital Audio Workstation or DAW, need to scan for new plugins. Open your music app and find the settings menu. Look for the plugins or VST folder section. Make sure the app scans the folder where you installed the Korg Collection. Once the scan finishes, you will see the instruments in your plugin list.

🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you do not see the instruments in your music software, check your plugin path settings again. Make sure your computer meets the minimum RAM requirements. If you hear clicks or pops, increase your audio buffer size in your music software settings. A larger buffer size reduces processing load and improves sound stability.

Is my sound card good enough?

Most modern internal sound cards work. However, a dedicated music interface provides better audio quality and lower delay.
